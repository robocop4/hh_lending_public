For partners - Four steps to enhance the security of your product using our platform.

Stay informed about vulnerabilities in your products before malicious actors exploit them.

  • Policy formulation. Define the goals of your bug bounty program using our template. What do you want to achieve, what vulnerabilities do you want to find? Determine the rewards and their amounts based on the type and severity of vulnerabilities.
  • Become our partner. Contact us via email to become our partner. In your email, attach the previously created document outlining the bug bounty program policy.
  • Set up your program. Follow simple steps to customize your bug bounty program.
  • Receive vulnerability reports. Participants from our community worldwide will study your product to identify and report any flaws in your system.
