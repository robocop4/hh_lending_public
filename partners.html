For partners - Proven solutions for real-world problems.

Our platform helps businesses communicate with information security specialists by providing direct access to qualified experts. We enable businesses to receive expert advice on enhancing the security of their information systems and data, as well as conducting audits and analyses to identify vulnerabilities. This helps prevent potential threats and minimize security risks, ultimately strengthening the business's defense mechanisms.

What we offer

Our platform is the link between your project and information security experts. Bug Bounty is a key feature of the HackeHub platform designed to mitigate risks by engaging a large group of ethical hackers who provide proactive and continuous monitoring of your evolving digital environment.

Our platform informs the hacker community about your readiness to collaborate on cybersecurity and provides tools for receiving and processing information about potential vulnerabilities. We provide ethical hackers with tools to document the vulnerability identification and assessment process, enabling you to react quickly and take steps to address the issue.

Bug bounty programs help to solve the following issues

  • Vulnerability detection
  • Threat elimination
  • Improving overall security
  • Improving image and credibility

Features

Using our platform, you have the capability to centrally manage received vulnerability reports, engage your colleagues in joint efforts to address shortcomings in your system. Here, you have direct communication with ethical hackers and can inform them about updates to your product as well as changes in your policy.
